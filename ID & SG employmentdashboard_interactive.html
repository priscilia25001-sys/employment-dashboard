Indonesia: 42% formal, 28.5% agriculture, 4.8% unemployment, 145.77M employed
Singapore: ~100% formal, 78% services, ~2-3% unemployment, ~3.8M employed
Bottom line: Indonesia emerging (formalize workforce), Singapore developed (sectoral reallocation amid automation)
